League Constitution

League Overview

BSFB is a true franchise building experience designed to mimic the NBA as closely as fantasy sports will allow.

Scoring System is Head-to-Head Total Points: Teams compete Head to Head to earn a single Win, Loss or Tie each week based on which team scores the most Fantasy Points in a scoring period. All 30 NBA Franchises and playes will be represented. Full 82 Game Schedule for 12 teams with 8 making the playoffs. Realistic year round off-season activity.

Our League began circa 2007-08 in the droms of Arizona State in Tempe. (In the same dorms of future NBA All-Star James Harden). This October we will be entering our 8th season as a league. Below is our "League Constitution", where all league rules and detailes will be housed.

Trades

Any trade can be made after the league draft concludes. Our trade deadline will always be the same as the NBA (usually the nearest Thursday following the All Star Break at 12pm Pacific Standard Time.

Weekly Trade Chat: Before the season begins we will vote on a night to hold weekly trade chats via the League Facebook Group Owners are not reqired to particpate in league chat, but it is highly recoomended to the ingretiy of the league. Any manager can also post the Facebook page of players that you are looking to trade and/or players/postions you need.

Trade Review: All current owners are memebers of the "Trade Review Committee". Any trade that happens during the season will be posted on the Facebook Group Page. If anyone feels the trade is biased or unfair in anyway they can veto the trade. If x amount of managers or % of the league (will vote on each season) veto's the trade with 72 hours it will be cancelled.

Lineup Management

Owners can set their own lineups, but active rosters lock five (5) minutes prior to tip-off of first game of scoring period.(Mon & Fri) Rosters can not be changed for scoring period once rosters lock.


Most leagues implement more strict guidlines when in comes to position eligiblity slots. Our league belives in more of "postionless" basketball aproach with roster spots.


Mangers are only required to fill one of each of the postions of the 10 max players that can be eligble each day. The next 2 slots are for any gaurd/forward and forward/center with enough crossover that should eliminate roster postion issues. The final 3 spots are FLEX postions that any player is eligble for.


Weekly Player Limit

Regular Season: 40 Players Max.

Playoffs/Post Season: 80 Players Max.

Each team can start a max of 40 players each week. Going over that limit before or after the week is over will result in the HIGHEST scoring player(s) being SUBTRACTED from your total score of the week. It is up to the managers to field exactly their best 40 to avoid the penalty and keeping the league fair.


If a player is placed in a roster spot in your lineup but does NOT play any minutes, then that player will not be added to your teams weekly total.


Please keep in mind that ometimes in league chat the player limit rule is also refered to as "The Lamar Reynolds Rule"



Draft Overview

The 2015-16 | Season VIII Draft will most likely be the last saturday or sunday in October around 9PM PST. We will confirm the official date when the NBA release's the schedule in August.

Type: Snake

Example: 1,2,3,4,5,6,5,4,3,2,1 etc.

Draft Location: Online ( Non Arizona Managers)

Live Draft Location: Rafa's Place Phoenix, AZ (TBA)

Time: The draft will start no earlier than 8pm and no later than 9pm. ESPN opens up the draft room exactly one hour before the draft starts. During this period it is highly reccommened to join the draft room early. It is important to test the ESPN Live Draft and contact a commisioner imediatally if you are having issues.

If ALL managers are in the draft room before 9pm we can start the draft early. Depending on time per pick the draft will last at least an hour but less than two usually.

Duration per Pick (Rounds 1-4): 120 Seconds

Duration per Pick (Rounds 5-13): 90 Seconds

Trading Picks: ARE allowed, but must be completed before the draft begins.

Voting for the league Hall of Fame will comense after Season 10 in 2017. All active/nactive managers are eligble to vote for 2 managers each season that meet the requirements. Voting will remain open for the summer with a max of two managers being inducted into the Hall of Fame each season.

    Yearly Awards

    Only 1 winner per catergory.
  • Rookie of the Year: All new managers are eligible to win this award that goes to the rookie that makes the deepest playoff run or best season record.

  • Most Improved Franchise Awarded to the manager that makes the biggest statistical jump between seasons.

  • Best Offensive Team Award goes the the team that leads in the most offensive categories at the end of the season. ( Points, Field Goals Made , Free Throws Made & Assists )

  • Best Defensive Team: Award goes the the team that leads in the most defensive categories at the end of the season. Defensive Rebounds Blocks Steals

  • Most Effcient Team: The team that leads the league in shooting % in FGM/FGA and FTM/FTA.

  • Most Fantasy Points (Season): This award goes to the team that has scored the most fantasy points over the entire regular season.

  • Most Fantasy Points (over 3 Seasons): This award goes to the team that has scored the most fantasy points over the entire regular season.

    (will vote on after Season 10)


    Before we reach our 10th Anniversary Season we wanted to have an award for Best Franchise over the next 3 seasons. Even if no team emerges as a true dynasty we also have a managers vote along with the following stats taken into account.
    (Championships,Finals Appearances, Total Wins/ Win %, Total Fantasy Points, Semi-Final Appearances, Playoff Appearances & Seasons completed.
    All active-inactive managers from seasons 7-10 are eligible to vote for best franchise. The managers vote will be factored in as 50% of the total vote with the other half going to the above statistics.


  • 10th Anniversary Dynasty Award We will also be doing the same thing as above but for the entire first 10 years of the league when we reach that milestone.
