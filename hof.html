Establishment

The leauge Hall of Fame will be implemented after the conclusion of Season X (2017-18). Any past/current manager is eligible to be inducted and/or vote for the Hall of Fame. Voting will open up in the summer of 2018, with a maximum of 2 managers being inducted into the hall each subsequent season.

To be eligible for the league Hall of Fame managers must meet the requirements of any of these three listed categories. Obvisously the more ways you are eligble the better the chances of being inducted.
